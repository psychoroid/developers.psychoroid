# Creating your merchant account

To start using psychoroid.com, you first need to create an account. 

1. Visit the [psychoroid.com website](https://psychoroid.com) and click on the "Sign Up" button.
2. Get through the KYC process and fill in your business details, including your company name, contact information, and the payment methods you want to support.
3. Agree to our terms of service.
4. You will receive a confirmation email. Click the link in the email to verify your account.

Congratulations! You have successfully created your psychoroid.com merchant account. You can now log in to your dashboard and start configuring your payment integration.
